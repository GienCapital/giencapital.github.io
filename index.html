What is AVΛTTARS?

AVΛTTARS is a dance-first music project. Instead of building everything around one singer, we built a universe where the movement, the masks and the atmosphere come first.

The AVΛTTARS voice is a stylized hybrid — created from our founder’s voice blended with AI vocal design. It gives the project one consistent vocal identity without putting a single person in the spotlight.

This lets AVΛTTARS grow as crews in many cities at the same time, all performing the same songs and shows in their own way. It’s not a traditional band. It’s a world you can join.

For dancers

Dancers

AVΛTTARS was built for dancers. Latex queens, high-heels queens, popping queens, neon wolves — the performers on stage and on screen are the real face of the project.

To join as a dancer:

  • Choose any AVΛTTARS track.
  • Create a 10–15 second choreography in your style.
  • Record it wearing a mask — your face stays anonymous.
  • Post on TikTok or Instagram with #AVATTARS and tag @avattars.official.
  • Send us the link with your city using the application form.

We are focusing first on Berlin, London, Warsaw, Tokyo, Seoul, Manila, New York, São Paulo and Paris — but any city can apply. As the music grows, so will the number of AVΛTTARS crews.

For vocalists

Singers

The core AVΛTTARS vocal identity is a hybrid: part founder, part AI. It lets the project stay anonymous, consistent and scalable.

At selected shows, we invite Guest Voices — singers and performers who add live elements on top of the main vocal track: spoken lines, vocoder phrases, hype shouts and chórki. Always masked.

To be considered as a Guest Voice:

  • Send a short voice note (spoken or sung).
  • Tell us your city and social links.
  • Let us know what kind of energy you bring on stage.

You don’t need to carry full songs. The hybrid voice is the lead — you help bring the show to life in the room.

For creators

Influencers

Influencers and creators can join the AVΛTTARS universe by posting masked content — lip syncs, transitions, dance snippets and edits to AVΛTTARS tracks.

Here’s how to take part:

  • Post a video using any AVΛTTARS song.
  • Wear a mask or partially cover your face.
  • Use hashtag #AVATTARS and tag @avattars.official.
  • Share your profiles with us through the creator form.

Active supporters may receive early access to new songs and challenges, VIP or early entry to shows, masks or merch for content, and priority when we build local crews.

For writers & producers

Songwriters

AVΛTTARS lives in a focused sound universe: dance pop, afro-influenced pop, house and modern electronic music built for movement.

We’re open to working with songwriters and producers who want to contribute hooks, toplines, lyrics or full demos that fit this world. Some tracks may be human-written, some AI-assisted — the goal is always the same: music that makes people move.

To send material:

  • Share private streaming links (no file attachments).
  • Include a short description of your role (lyrics, topline, production).
  • Mention if you prefer to be credited or stay anonymous.
