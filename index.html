Brand Story

What is AVΛTTARS?

AVΛTTARS was created by dancers, for dancers. It’s a global masked dance collective where movement, anonymity and atmosphere matter more than ego or celebrity.

Dance comes first

The show, not the singer

In a normal band, the vocalist is the star and everyone else supports them. AVΛTTARS flips this model: here, the voice is part of the world — but the dancers are the ones in the spotlight.

Our songs are written for choreography, TikTok hooks and live performances. Every track is built so dancers can move, cut, loop and reinvent it.

A hybrid vocal identity

The AVΛTTARS voice

To keep the focus off one “star singer”, the AVΛTTARS voice is a stylized hybrid created from our founder’s voice blended with AI vocal design. It’s one consistent identity, but it doesn’t belong to any single person.

This lets crews perform in many cities at the same time, to the same songs, without needing a touring vocalist.

Why we’re different

Not a normal band

AVΛTTARS isn’t a traditional artist project. It’s a scalable performance universe built around dance, masks and global crews.

Dancers are the stars

No frontperson. No solo singer. No single “face” of the project. The stars are the masked dancers — Latex Queens, High Heels Queens, Popping Queens and Neon Wolves — bringing the music to life on stage and on screen.

Global, not local

AVΛTTARS is designed for multiple city crews. Berlin, London, Warsaw, Tokyo, New York and more — all performing AVΛTTARS shows, sometimes on the same night, with their own local dancers and the same signature sound.

The show is everything

The music matters — but the live shows are the heart of it. Masked choreography, neon lighting, chórki from dancers, spoken lines, vocoder moments and high-energy formations turn every concert into a cinematic dance ritual.

Join our dance crew

Want to perform as an official AVΛTTARS dancer in your city? This is how you enter the universe.

How it works

Steps to apply

  • Choose any AVΛTTARS track that moves you.
  • Create a 10–15 second choreography in your style.
  • Record it wearing a mask — your face must stay anonymous.
  • Post on TikTok or Instagram with #AVATTARS and @avattars.official.
  • Send us the link via the “Join Crew” form, choosing your city.

First wave of recruitment will focus on Berlin, London, Warsaw, Tokyo, New York and Manila, with more cities opening soon.

Creators

Become an AVΛTTARS Siren

Sirens are influencers and creators who help grow the universe using lip syncs, masked edits, transitions and dance snippets to AVΛTTARS tracks.

For influencers & creators

How to become a Siren

  • Post a lip sync, transition or dance video to any AVΛTTARS song.
  • Wear a mask or partially obscure your face — anonymity is part of the world.
  • Use hashtag #AVATTARSSIREN and tag @avattars.official.
  • Apply through the “Siren Program” form with your profile links.
Perks

What Sirens receive

  • Priority access to new songs and challenges.
  • Invitations to AVΛTTARS shows with VIP or early entry.
  • Custom masks or merch for content creation.
  • Chance to be featured on the official channels.
  • Priority consideration for joining a city dance crew.
Stage roles

Become a guest voice at concerts

At selected shows, we invite Guest Voices — creators, singers and performers — to add live spoken lines, chórki and vocoder moments on stage, always masked.

On stage

Your role as Guest Voice

  • Speak iconic AVΛTTARS lines into vocoder or FX mics.
  • Add short chórki and hype shouts on top of the main track.
  • Lead call-and-response moments with the crowd.
  • Stay masked — identity is part of the universe.

You don’t have to carry full songs. The main vocal is our hybrid voice — you add live energy on top.
